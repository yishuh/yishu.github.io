About Durm Schtik

Durm Schtik is a sophomore at Tufts University where he studies Economics and Computer Science. When studying in a summer program at Peking University, He discovered his special interests in international monetary economics. In both high school and university, he has been working with NGOs as volunteers and gained experience communicating with children with cognitive disabilities and students from low-income families in China. As one of the designers of the Tufts Observer, He dedicates his free time to multimedia and arts. He also enjoys playing ultimate frisbee and going to shoegazing concerts.
